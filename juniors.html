Juniors (English 11, AP Language & AP Seminar)

Disclaimer: The following is the curriculum that all students in English 11, AP Language and Composition, and AP Seminar follow including the skills that are meant to be acquired in the school year. This section presents how AI can be used as a tool to advance the curriculum and instances where students may be able to use software in their assignments. Please ensure that AI is being used as a tool in your academic work, not as software abused to do an entire assignment for you. Academic integrity and the respective use of AI is our main goal.

Recommended AIs

NotebookLM, Otter.ai, Doctrina, AskCodi, Kickresume, Gamma AI, PDF AI, Leonardo AI

  • NotebookLM: AI note-taking and summarization.
  • Otter.ai: Transcription for lecture review.
  • Doctrina: Organize notes and quizzes.
  • AskCodi: Coding assistance.
  • Kickresume: Resume and cover letter help.
  • Gamma AI: AI-driven presentations.
  • PDF AI: Interact with PDFs for summarization.
  • Leonardo AI: AI art for creative projects.

When can I Use AI?

AI can be a great tool for 11th-grade students to boost productivity and enhance learning. It helps with organizing notes, creating study aids, and even formatting citation. AI can handle repetitive tasks and provide instant feedback, allowing students to focus more on understanding and exploring subjects deeper. However, it's essential to use AI as a helper and not a cheat. Students should always make sure that their work remains original and genuinely reflects their own understanding and creativity.
