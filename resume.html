Resume Writing

Recommended AIs — Keep in mind that these are only suggestions. Students are open to using all AI tools listed in the "AI Usage Guidelines" section under "Which AI Tools are Acceptable?" Teachers are also welcome to guide their students in a certain direction. All tools must be used with the permission and discretion of a student's teacher. Misuse of AI will result in consequences.

Recommended Tools & Uses

  • QuillBot — grammar checking and restructuring; helpful for cover letters (use sparingly for bullet points).
  • PDF AI — summarize long job postings to tailor applications.
  • Grammarly AI — grammar checks and AI Resume Skills Generator for bullet points.
  • Canva AI — design templates and AI art (use with discretion for creative fields).

Components of a Resume

  1. Contact information: Top of document, professional and easy to find.
  2. Objective: 1–3 sentences of qualifications and goals (or professional summary for experienced users).
  3. Skills: 7–10 bullets; tailor to job description.
  4. Work Experience: Position, employer, dates, and quantifiable achievements.
  5. Education: Keep high school on until college graduation.

Additional elements

Awards & honors, languages, GPA (include scale), and links to templates (e.g., Google Docs, Indeed).

For any more information, check out indeed.com for specialized advice, and feel free to use Google Docs templates for resumes!
