Seniors (English 12, AP Literature & AP Research)

Disclaimer: The following is the curriculum that all students in English 12, AP Literature and Composition, and AP Research follow including the skills that are meant to be acquired in the school year. This section presents how AI can be used as a tool to advance the curriculum and instances where students may be able to use software in their assignments. Please ensure that AI is being used as a tool in your academic work, not as software abused to do an entire assignment for you. Academic integrity and the respective use of AI is our main goal.

Recommended AIs

NotebookLM, Otter.ai, Doctrina, AskCodi, Kickresume, Gamma AI, PDF AI, Leonardo AI

English 12 Curriculum

Core English Courses: English 4 (Academic, Accelerated, Honors) — Emphasizes college-level writing and critical analysis. Focuses on nonfiction and fiction to prepare students for post-secondary education.

AP Curriculum

AP English Literature and Composition

Purpose: Engages students in careful reading and critical analysis of literature. Prepares students for the AP exam in May.

AP Research

Purpose: Builds on skills from AP Seminar to develop an independent, college-level research project. Encourages students to explore real-world topics and present original arguments.

Elective Curriculum

  • Creative Writing 2 — advanced writing and stylistic development.
  • Journalism and Media — fundamentals of journalism, news writing, and publishing projects.

When can I Use AI?

AI can be a great tool for 12th-grade students to boost productivity and enhance learning. It helps with organizing notes, creating study aids, and even formatting citation. AI can handle repetitive tasks and provide instant feedback, allowing students to focus more on understanding and exploring subjects deeply. However, it's essential to use AI as a helper and not a cheat. Students should always make sure that their work remains original and genuinely reflects their own understanding and creativity.
